Starknet, an Ethereum Layer-2 network, launched the Runes bridge. A new Starknet <> Bitcoin bridge allowing Bitcoin users to access non-BTC assets like USDC directly on the Bitcoin network.

This initiative leverages the Rune meta-protocol on Bitcoin, enabling 1:1 redeemability of assets on Starknet.

## Facts about Asset Runes

- **Launched:** May 2025
- **Protocol:** Rune meta-protocol on Bitcoin
- **Challenges:**
  - Complexity of the Bitcoin & Runes protocol
  - Building strong security model 
  - Working in coherence with a confederation of Bitcoin actors for security

## Key Features

- **Native Bitcoin Assets:** Access non-BTC assets without leaving Bitcoin's security
- **Runes Trading on Starknet:** Access runes BTC on Starknet to facilitate trading
- **Security-proof Bridge:** Secured by prominent Bitcoin and blockchain entities like Xverse
